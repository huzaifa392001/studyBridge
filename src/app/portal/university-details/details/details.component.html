Introduction

An autonomous and convergent education system to strengthen creativity and personality education. It provides a variety of academic access opportunities with the aim of introducing a convergence L-shaped education system that lowers the barriers between majors, exploring interdisciplinary topics, and cultivating cooperative practical skills through critical and creative thinking and in-depth discussions. It helps complement various perspectives through in-depth discussions of student autonomous curriculum, student design major, and convergence subject courses. Reading seminar to cultivate personality

Mission

Training talents who are going on a new path together.
A bold truth exploration and cooperative research on social challenges.
Academic community that respects ethics and diversity and communicates.

Academic Goals

By discovering various social contribution activities, we cultivate talented people who practice sharing and serve. Through participation in social contributions linked to education, we can provide opportunities to experience contribution activities and contribute widely to society. Through these various activities, students understand diversity by learning the concepts of volunteering, global citizens, and human rights, and grow into "Shanum-type (Seoul National University + Sharing)" talents with a cool-headed reason and warm heart.

Eligability and requirements

– Person who has obtained a bachelor's degree or who is expected to obtain it.
– English grades: TEPS (327 points/based on NewTeps) or higher, TOEFL IBT–86 points or higher (exempt if you graduate from a foreign English-speaking university)
– The format of reading sociology-related texts in English and describing answers (answer can be both English and Korean) – impossible to bring in advance, and failure without exception if missed.
– The scores of the major written exam are used as reference materials for interviews and oral examinations.
– Level 2, interview and oral exam.
